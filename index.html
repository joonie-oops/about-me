Latest Project

Tindog

Tindog is the perfect social network for dogs to meet and mingle! Tindog allows owners to create a profile for their furry friends, complete with photos, bios, and interests. Users can swipe through profiles of other dogs in their area and "match" with ones they'd like to set up a playdate with. The app also offers dog-friendly recommendations for parks, cafes, and other fun spots to visit with your pup. Tindog is a fun and easy way for dogs to socialize and make new friends, and for owners to connect with like-minded dog lovers in their community.
